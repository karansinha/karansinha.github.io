Water Supply
  • Automatic Water Supply
    The innovative concept of non electrical pump, such Pumps don’t need any kind of electricity and it is cheap in price. Small farmers are getting benefited by this pump. IEDI pumps are made for cost reduction and increasing the production of farmer. The organization has helped one million farming households spread over 17 states increase their annual income! IDEI is now exporting its technologies to countries in Africa and Southeast Asia, aiming to reach 30 million people by 2020. Similar to IEDI Kickstart is an African agri company which has hold in Africa. It is very cheap and it is increasing the production in farm. It is mainly given to women to start farming by themselves. This project can be implemented in India. The Moneymaking pump is without electricity and it has very innovative concept.
    FlyBird products are very smart in use, less electricity supply, helps to increase production in systematic way, reduces labor cost such innovation need to expand in Indian farming for better results in quality and production.
    Water supply for Mango, Pomegranate, grapes is difficult. To tackle this issue MITRA produced and designed special innovative machine which has been very successful in Maharashtra. MITRA has helped many farmers to do farming easily and in smart way. The production and quality of farm has increased after using MITRA machines.
  • Innovative Water Supply Machines
    MITRA water spray machine, Mitra has developed very effective machines to spray water to Grapes,Mango,Orange and other big tree fruits. MITRA machine is widely used by Farmers and it has good results. It is mainly used in Maharashtra and the government is taking initiative to promote such machines to the farmers by exhibition. It helps to reduce employment and the work is done very fast.

    Pomemaster Rocket+ - As the name suggests, is an air blast sprayer specially developed for Pomegranates farms. It is one of its kind, tractor mounted sprayer which can easily work on 18 HP and above tractor. It easily provides highest output of 15,966m3/hr on 18 HP tractor. Compact, lightweight, quality designs are its hallmarks. The reverse fan system helps to avoid the suction of leaves, grass and mulching residuals from the ground. The computer balanced fan comes with counter veins that ensures uniform air output. This results in evenly sized export quality yield.

    Grape Master- MITRA designs and manufactures world class "Orchard and vineyard Sprayers". These sprayers can be trailed or mounted on tractors thus being recognized as tractor trailed sprayers, tractor mounted sprayers. Air Blast technology is being used in these sprayers to provide sufficient air output for Grape orchards/vineyards. Air blast sprayers produce mist of fine droplet sizes which are optimized for delivery of plant protection and plant enhancement chemicals. Thus they are also recognized as Mist blowers. MITRA uses axial fans specially suited for different sizes of canopy and air Assisted Sprayer technology works as atomizer and provided complete coverage.

Cold Storage - Quality Test
  • Packaging and Lab Testing
    CORALENE antifog Packaging- LEAF's all products are packaged in the CORALENE antifog film, which is a Bi-oriented, co-extruded polyprop-ylene film for wrapping fruit and vegetable using flowpack packaging machines. The CORALENE antifog complies with EU and FDA regulations and the anti-fog effect is obtained without using solvents or lacquers based on PVC or PVdC. Branding - Marketing - Selling
  • Modern Organised e-Mandi
    StarAgri Mandi- Star Agri aims to improve farmer access to markets by building modern, organized agri trade and marketing infrastructure across India. This means establishing electronic trading platforms and modern rural retail and wholesale markets that are efficient, professionally managed and responsive to farmer’s needs. With its first-of-its-kind private ‘mandi’ coming up in Kota, Rajasthan, StarAgri aims to create 50 agri trade markets licensed by the Agricultural Produce Marketing Committees (APMCs) that serve as information, innovation and trade centres for agri produce. An efficient market place where sellers, buyers, and end customers converge, Star Agri state-of-the-art ‘mandis’ will provide a modern shopping-mall-like infrastructure that is not available in traditional markets. With clean, hygienic and green environments, StarAgri’s modern ‘mandis’ will provide one-stop services with the quality and transparency of international standards.
  • Direct to farmer
    A "direct-to-farmer" m-commerce platform AgroStar aims to transform Agri-business for farmers in rural India. AgroStar has built a “direct to farmer” m-commerce platform through which farmers can procure agri-inputs needed for their farms by simply giving a missed call on platform and eventually accessing mobile app. AgroStar’s "direct to farmer" m-commerce platform has become the preferred choice for farmers to buy good quality agri-inputs which are delivered to their doorstep.
